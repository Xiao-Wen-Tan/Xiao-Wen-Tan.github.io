Xiaowen Tan

In it together: employees, firms, and the values they share.

I am a fourth-year strategy PhD student at The University of Texas at Dallas. My research explores how social values carried by social movements, firm initiatives, and culture shape employees' attitudes and behaviors, and how employees as agentic stakeholders impact organizations.
